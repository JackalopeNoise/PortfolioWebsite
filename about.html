About Me

My name is Jario Gutierrez! I'm a Digital Arts Student at Kent University in Canterbury, working towards someday getting a job in illustration, whether that's on a comic or concept artist in the video game industry. My main motivation is the drive to want to create the things that are always forming in my head, concepts and characters, animations, I want to reach a point where I can put any idea I have to the paper. I'm mostly focused on Digital Illustration, but I have knowledge on video editing, 3d modelling and photography! I've done a fair bit of professional independent work for people, but I'm always willing to learn more! I prefer to use my mascot character to represent me in my stead,
